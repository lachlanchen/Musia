# Producer Brief - 梦游天姥吟留别

Create a beautiful Mandarin art-pop / xianxia ballad from selected lines of Li
Bai's `梦游天姥吟留别`.

## Structure

1. Ascend: `脚著谢公屐 / 身登青云梯`.
2. Vision: `半壁见海日 / 空中闻天鸡`.
3. Waking loss: `惟觉时之枕席 / 失向来之烟霞`.
4. Philosophy: `世间行乐亦如此 / 古来万事东流水`.
5. Freedom: `安能摧眉折腰事权贵 / 使我不得开心颜`.

## Sound

Female lead vocal, cinematic Chinese xianxia art-pop ballad, piano, guqin, pipa,
dizi, warm strings, deep drums, airy choir pads, spacious mountain dream
atmosphere. The chorus should feel open, defiant, and liberating rather than
like a classroom recitation.
