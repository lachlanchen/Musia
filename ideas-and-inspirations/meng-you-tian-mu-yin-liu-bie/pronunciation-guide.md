# 梦游天姥吟留别 Pronunciation Guide

Use this guide before ACE generation and again during ASR/listening correction.

## Core Readings

- `梦游天姥吟留别`: `mèng yóu tiān mǔ yín liú bié`.
- `天姥`: `tiān mǔ`, never `tiān lǎo`.
- `脚著谢公屐`: `jiǎo zhuó xiè gōng jī`.
- `身登青云梯`: `shēn dēng qīng yún tī`.
- `半壁见海日`: `bàn bì jiàn hǎi rì`.
- `空中闻天鸡`: `kōng zhōng wén tiān jī`.
- `惟觉时之枕席`: `wéi jué shí zhī zhěn xí`.
- `失向来之烟霞`: `shī xiàng lái zhī yān xiá`.
- `世间行乐亦如此`: `shì jiān xíng lè yì rú cǐ`.
- `古来万事东流水`: `gǔ lái wàn shì dōng liú shuǐ`.
- `且放白鹿青崖间`: `qiě fàng bái lù qīng yá jiān`.
- `须行即骑访名山`: `xū xíng jí qí fǎng míng shān`.
- `安能摧眉折腰事权贵`: `ān néng cuī méi zhé yāo shì quán guì`.
- `使我不得开心颜`: `shǐ wǒ bù dé kāi xīn yán`.

## Model-Facing Notes

- Put `天姥 tian1 mu3`, `脚著 jiao3 zhuo2`, and `须行即骑 xu1 xing2 ji2 qi2`
  directly in the ACE caption.
- Do not include pinyin in the lyric file itself.
- Do not sing title, author, section labels, or pronunciation notes.
- Preserve source text when ASR only gives a sound-close guess with the same
  phrase length.
