# Mars Desert Song Brief

Date: 2026-06-28

Working title: **Small Under the Red Sky**

## User Direction

Create a broad, moving song set in a desert on Mars. It should feel vast, open, lonely, and human: a person senses their smallness against the universe, but the sadness turns into forward motion and courage.

Target feeling:

- 广阔 / 辽阔
- 打动人心
- 鼓舞人
- 悲伤中有奋进
- 感受自己的渺小和宇宙的广远

## Research Anchors

- NASA describes Mars as a dusty, cold desert world with a very thin atmosphere, seasons, polar ice caps, extinct volcanoes, canyons, and weather.
- NASA notes Mars was once wetter and warmer, which gives the lyric a “lost ocean / ancient water” emotional layer without over-explaining it.
- Valles Marineris is a huge canyon system about 3,870 km long and up to 9.3 km deep, useful as an image of scale.
- Olympus Mons is the largest volcano in the solar system, more than 40 km tall from base to summit, useful as an image of impossible height.
- NASA frames Mars as a horizon goal for human exploration and a place that can teach us about Earth, life, and human possibility.

Sources:

- https://science.nasa.gov/mars/facts/
- https://science.nasa.gov/mars/
- https://science.gsfc.nasa.gov/690/solar-system-tour/Mars.html
- https://science.nasa.gov/resource/valles-marineris-the-grand-canyon-of-mars/

## Production Direction

Use ACE-Step full-song generation first.

Common settings:

- Duration: 82 seconds
- BPM: 82
- Key: E minor
- Time: 4/4
- Form: verse, pre-chorus, chorus
- Texture: cinematic desert pop / post-rock ballad
- Arrangement: low synth drone, sparse piano, warm strings, distant choir pad, huge but clean drums, electric guitar swells, sub bass
- Vocal: clear upfront expressive vocal, no real singer imitation, connected phrases, no clipped endings

## Lyric Strategy

Use short, image-heavy lines. Avoid overloaded syllables because generated vocals become less intelligible when lines are too dense. Each language version should keep:

- red dust / red sky
- blue sunset or blue sun through dust
- canyon scale
- human smallness
- a spark/fire/heart rising
- moving forward despite cosmic silence

The three language versions are adaptations. They do not need word-for-word equality; each vocal render gets its own trilingual lyric set on the website.
