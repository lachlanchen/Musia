# 陌上桑 Quick Reference for the User

`陌上桑` is also known as `罗敷行` / `日出东南隅行`, a Han Yuefu narrative poem
about Qin Luofu picking mulberry leaves. Its famous opening is:

```text
日出东南隅，照我秦氏楼。
秦氏有好女，自名为罗敷。
罗敷喜蚕桑，采桑城南隅。
```

For the `沙沙` song, I used it only as a cultural texture: sunlight, a woman by
the road/field, a distant gaze, and classical softness. The actual song remains
about beach sand, desert sand, two people walking by the sea, and sunset.
