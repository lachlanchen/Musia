# 沙沙 Producer Brief

Route: ACE-Step original Mandarin song, soft beach ballad.

## Emotional Arc

Two people walk on a beach at sunset. The song begins with tiny grains of sand,
then opens into sea/desert distance, a dream wind from Chiba to Loulan, and ends
quietly with the two of them going home under the sun.

The feeling should be soft, beautiful, intimate, warm, and slightly wistful. It
should not become a grand war song, folk recitation, or dense poem-song.

## Lyric Density

The lyric is intentionally short-line Chinese with repeated `沙沙` hooks. The
model should leave breath after phrase endings and hold vowels on `沙沙`, `浪花`,
`晚霞`, and `回家`.

## Arrangement

- Soft cinematic Mandarin C-pop ballad.
- 76 BPM, F major, 4/4.
- Acoustic guitar fingerpicking, warm piano, soft strings, light ocean-like percussion, very gentle bass.
- Clear upfront expressive Mandarin vocal, tender female lead with airy backing harmony allowed.
- No real-singer imitation.

## Negative Guidance

- Do not sing title/author/source notes.
- Do not speak credits or model notes.
- No rap, no spoken recitation.
- No clipped final words.
- Do not bury the vocal under pads.
- Do not cram every line into fast syllables.
